Browser-based jailbreaks

TotallyNotSpyware (semi-untethered) (10.0-10.3.3) (64-bit)
Carbon (untethered*) (8.0-9.3.6) (32-bit)
Lyncis (untethered) (7.1-7.1.2) (32-bit)
JailbreakMe Saffron (untethered) (4.2.6-4.3.3) (32-bit)
JailbreakMe Star (untethered) (3.1.2-3.2.1) (32-bit)

*: On 9.3.5-9.3.6 you must install iocaste untether from cydia to keep it untethered


If your version is not covered by these browser-based jailbreaks, refer to iOS CFW guide (may not work on legacy iOS devices).
